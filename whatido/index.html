What I Do

Well, as you can see from below, I do a little bit of everything. If you don't currently have a website, I'll get you going with something you'll love whether it's a small one-page brochure style website or a fully-loaded dynamic website with login capabilities. And if you've already got something that you wanted touched up, I can do that too!

What exactly can I do for you though, you ask? Here's a few ideas:

  • Help you purchase your domain name (i.e. yourbusinessname.com)
  • Get you a website like this (or better!!) online so that you can have a presence (I mean, it is 2022).
  • Need a database too? I got you covered!
  • Need it to pull some information from that database or another data source? That's where my knowledge of PHP and other "backend" languages like Java and C# come into play.
  • How about something you can edit yourself after I'm done? I'll help you get started with a WordPress site!

I also love cybersecurity. One of my goals is to not only get you a beautiful website and online presence and whatever you're looking for but I also want you to feel secure. There are always news stories of websites and companies getting hacked and I want to help you avoid being the next story in the local news by using the most up-to-date methods and knowledge to keep you secure. While I'm at it, part of my testing strategy will be to run vulnerability tests on your new website to make sure I didn't forget anything!
