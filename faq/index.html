Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a website?

If you're running a business, no matter how big or small, these days it's important to have as much of a web presence as possible.

Check out this article. It sums up why you should have a website in 2022.

How much is a website?

That actually dependeds on what you want for your website. This is actually a fairly loaded question. Put simply, the more you want your website to do, the more it will cost. For example, a simple one-page brochure style website is going to cost much less than an e-commerce website for you to sell your store's items online!

Can you help me update an old website?

You bet I can! If you have a website you feel needs updating, get in touch and we can discuss how I can fix it up for you and get you looking professional and modern!

Is there any funding to help me out with the cost?

If you have operate a small business out of Prince Edward Island, Innovation PEI has a wonderful program to help you get a web presence. Check it out here!

The main points are:

  1. You must not already have a web presence (website).
  2. Helps to pay for domain registration, hosting fees, and development of website (what I do!).
  3. 50% of the associated costs for a website are covered up to $1000 for non-e-commerce and up to $2500 for an e-commerce site.
  4. Not available to upgrade your existing website.

How will Google find my website?

Lots of ways! The main way is something called "search engine optimization" or SEO, for short.

SEO is something I can help you with by using something called meta tags in between the head tags. Using a variety of descriptors such as simple descriptions and keywords, Google will use their own automated bots to search the web and find your website. Another thing that helps is putting your business on Google using Google Business, which I can help you out with!
