About Me

Back in the days of dial-up internet, Logan MacKinnon can still remember sitting at his mother's desktop computer using basic HTML to create a new Geocities website every day. These things just came natural to him and when the time came to make the decision of what he wanted as a career, programming and web development was a no-brainer.

While the early days of HTML were learned in after school programs, the true design knowledge and the progression into backend development came from a formal education in the Computer Information Systems program at Holland College. It was here that Logan really gained a true passion for information technology and how to create applications big and small.

Besides programming and web development, Logan is Programmer Analyst with Veterans Affairs Canada, a father to two beautiful little girls, and partner to an amazing lady. Logan also spends much of his free time (what little he has having two kids) with volunteer web management, watching too much Star Trek and catching the latest Marvel movie. He has also found a love for all things IT security for which he holds the eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester certification.
